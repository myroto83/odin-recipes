Ribs

    Remove membrane from the bone of the ribs
    Brine with salt and keep refrigerated for 12-24 hours
    Bring to room temperature and dust with your favorite rub
    Place in smoker at 225-250 degrees for 6-8 hours
    Baste with your favorite bbq sauce and increase temp to 275 for 5min
    Pull from the smoker and let rest for 10min
